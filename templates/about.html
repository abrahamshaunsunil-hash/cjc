History

Abraham Industries was officially founded on February 6, 2024, born from a deep understanding of a major challenge faced by countless startups—failure due to weak marketing strategies, inefficient data handling, and the lack of scalable tech solutions. Witnessing how promising businesses with the potential to transform the country were falling short, Abraham Shaun Sunil from India, set out to build something revolutionary. He recognized that many ventures, despite having strong ideas, struggled to grow because they lacked the tools and insights to operate efficiently in a competitive environment. To solve this, Shaun envisioned an AI-powered solution that would empower startups and businesses by managing their data, marketing, and operational processes through a centralized, intelligent system. With co-founders Alwin Devasia and Ansuman Bhattacharjee, Abraham Industries was created as a B2B technology company offering AI models installed directly into client infrastructures. These models not only streamline business tasks but also offer guidance and training, ensuring startups can grow sustainably. The long-term vision is to scale this model globally, becoming a trusted partner in startup success across borders.
